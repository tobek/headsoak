What Smart Tags Can Do
Extract insights from your notes. Use the auto tag to automatically tag your notes with keywords. Or the sentiment tag to see where your notes fall on the positive and negative spectrum.
Perform actions on your notes. Use the remember this tag to reap the benefits of spaced repetition learning. We'll email you an hour, a day, a month, and a year after you create a note to help you internalize what you wrote down. Tag a note that's important only short-term with the self destruct tag. After 30 days they'll no longer crowd your notes.
